✦ Edit App Tutorial

Add Reels
in Order

Follow these 6 simple steps to add your Instagram Reels in exactly the order you want inside the Edit app.

1

Update the Edit App

Go to the App Store or Play Store and update Edit to the latest version.

2

Open Edit App

Launch the Edit app and open an existing project or start a New Project.

3

Tap on "Links"

In the bottom toolbar, tap the Links button (Instagram icon).

4

Select "Reels"

A popup appears with options — tap on Reels to continue.

5

Go to "My Reels" Tab

Tap the My Reels tab to see all your Instagram Reels listed.

6

Tap in the Order You Want ✅

Tap each reel one by one in the exact order you want them in your video. Done!

🔑

Key Tip

The order you tap = order in the timeline. Tap your first reel first, second reel second — and they'll appear exactly like that!
